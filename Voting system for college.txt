Voting system for college
1 - signup/sign in--voter 
2 - list of candidate
3 - voter can vote once
4 - live vote counts with candidate list
5 - candidate voting screen
6 - one admin who has access to add candidate
7 - forget password/reset password
8 - admin and candidate can't vote

Routes-

Auth->
       /signup--Post
       /login---Post
Voting->
      /candidates-list---GET
      /vote/:candidateID---POST
Counts-->
     /vote-counts---GET
Profile-->
     /profile----GET
     /profile/password---PUT
ADMIN--->
     /candidates----POST
     /candidates/:candidateId-----PUT(to update the candidate)
     /candidate/:candidateId------DELETE
